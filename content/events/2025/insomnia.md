## About Insomnia  

**Insomnia** is the flagship competitive programming contest of **MNNIT Allahabad**, where logic meets endurance and teamwork meets strategy. Conducted as part of **Avishkar**, our annual tech fest, Insomnia brings together programmers from across the country to compete in a high-pressure, high-energy coding marathon.  

Inspired by the **ACM-ICPC style**, the contest challenges teams to think fast, code smart, and stay sharp till the very last minute.  

---  

## What is Insomnia?  

Insomnia is a **3-hour ICPC-style team programming contest** hosted on **Codeforces**, where teams of up to three members solve a curated set of algorithmic problems under strict time and penalty constraints.
